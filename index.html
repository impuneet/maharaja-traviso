Our story

Love for food

INDIANO MAHARAJA is an Indian restaurant in the heart of Italy in Treviso..With us you can enjoy the authentic taste of North and South India. As one of the few Indian restaurants in Treviso, besides the North Indian curries, biryani's, rotis we also offer the well-known South-Indian dishes such as Dosa.
